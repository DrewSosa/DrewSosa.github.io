About. I am a recent graduate of Dartmouth College, where I majored in Physics & Government. I wear a few hats. I work in science & technology policy at the Day One Project, and I work as an independent AI researcher. I care about breaking down barriers in science, and making sure that the best ideas and best people get to the decision table. I'll be posting my science work and my policy work in this blog.

Outside of research, I like learning new languages, nerding out about military history, hooping, and thinking about how we can work towards a better tomorrow.
